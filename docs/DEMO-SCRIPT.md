## Global Archaeology Hub — Demo Script (EN)

### Opening pitch

**Tsianakas:** First, we will present our innovation project: the **Global Archaeology Hub** app. Archaeology is not only about excavating objects, but about investigating and safeguarding our culture. Today, protecting cultural heritage relies on technology, but we found the necessary tools are scattered across different devices and unorganized applications—creating chaos. Communication is also difficult because everyone prefers different platforms.

**Alexandros:** Our vision is for Global Archaeology Hub to become a foundation for the **digital preservation of the world’s cultural heritage.** We want to enable every country—regardless of resources—to record and showcase its culture with modern tools. Our goal is for the app to be adopted by organizations and universities as an educational tool, and to strengthen interaction between professionals and archaeology enthusiasts.

**Kipros:** The project was demanding, but it was completed successfully. We formed the team based on our skills. Through the competition, we gained valuable knowledge—from prompt engineering to web-app architecture. We believe we created an application that can make a difference.

### Q&A — 4 likely questions (with strong answers)

**1) “How does the app work offline? At archaeological sites there’s often no signal.”**  
**Answer:** Great point. Using Supabase, we can implement **local caching**. Data is saved on the device first and, once the user regains connectivity, the app **automatically syncs** to the cloud. This way, no information is lost—even in remote locations.

**2) “How do you ensure data from amateurs is valid and won’t fill your database with mistakes?”**  
**Answer:** We designed a **two-level system**. Professional data is stored in **Verified Layers**, while community posts go into a separate layer. Additionally, our AI can help filter submissions before they reach the database, and professionals can also review and evaluate public discoveries.

**3) “There are other tools out there. What is your Unique Selling Point (USP)?”**  
**Answer:** Our advantage is **integration**. Most apps focus on either mapping or communication. We combine **AI analysis**, **2D technical drawing**, and a **global community** into a single platform—also **free** and **open source**.

**4) “How will you fund the app if it’s free?”**  
**Answer:** Our goal isn’t profit from the user—it’s heritage preservation. We plan to pursue **grants** from cultural organizations and universities. We could also offer an **Enterprise** version for large-scale state excavations, while keeping the core tools always free for students and the public.
